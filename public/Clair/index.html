About Clair

We are a team of undergraduate engineering students from NSUT associated with Enactus, an international organization whose entrepreneurial-based projects are aimed at empowering people by transforming opportunities into real sustainable progress for the society. Hence we have come up with a solution to the problem of rapidly increasing pollution levels in India.

Clean Air Made Affordable

CLAIR is a low cost, high- efficiency electric air purifier specially designed to combat Delhi’s air pollution through an innovative two-step purification technology consisting of a pre-filter and a HEPA filter. Its high affordability and exceptional performance makes it the ultimate choice to keep your loved ones safe from indoor air pollution.

Two Stage Purification Technology

CLAIR effectively removes particles which are less than 0.3 microns in diameter by using a two-stage purification process.

1. PRE FILTER : The pre-filter is instrumental in capturing large sized particles like dust, hair, fur etc. It protects the HEPA filter from premature choking and thus extends its life.

2. HEPA FILTER : The HEPA (High Efficiency Particulate Arrestor) filters are capable of capturing 95% of the particulate matter. The H12 grade HEPA used in CLAIR captures 99.97% of all particles, including the deadly PM 2.5, 10 and 1, pollens, viruses, bacteria etc.
